Jin Woo Oh

Mechanical engineer turned software and data engineer — I build data systems end to end and measure them honestly.

My path into software started in the lab. I trained as a mechanical engineer and spent my research years on soft robotics and 3D printing — work that taught me to build things that have to survive contact with the real world, instrument them carefully, and let the data decide what's actually working.

I've since carried that same instinct into data science and engineering: designing pipelines, building and validating models, and writing the software that ties them together. What I care about most are systems that stay honest about their own performance — that measure themselves the way you'd measure a physical prototype. The work below is where that shows up.
